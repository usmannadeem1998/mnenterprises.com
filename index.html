About Us

M.N.Enterprises is in business for last 35 years. We are professional in our field. We deal in CAD designing and CNC machining of all type of injection and blow molding molds. Specialized in manufacturing of injection molded shells and blow molded plastic tubes including battery casing and blasting accessories. M.N.Enterprises also produces the commercial grade electrical cabels and explosive detonator wires including copper rod, wire drawing, annealing and Tin coating.
